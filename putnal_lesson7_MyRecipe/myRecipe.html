Scallops, Speghetti, and Okra

Ingredients

  • 1 stick butter
  • olive oil
  • white wine
  • minced garlic
  • parsley
  • okra
  • progresso clam sauce
  • 1/2 - 1 full pound of scallops
  • sea salt

Instructions

Melt a small amount of butter in a medium siced pan

use tin foil over bake pan, set okra on tin foil

add olive oil, garlic powder, etc.,

cook at 425 for 15 minutes and then broil for another 5 or so, cook pasta

Add butter to the pan

when the butter is melted add a small amount of olive oil, add garlic powder, sea salt, lemon pepper, and parsley to the scallops,

stir in white wine and scallops, cook for 3 minutes
